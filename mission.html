About Us

Our mission is to nurture health to promote welfare of individuals and families by providing compassionate and comprehensive patient-centered quality home health care that is safe and dependable.


At Guardian Angel Health Agency, we are committed to providing our clients with holistic, patient-centered care that is staff-driven and tailored to meet our clients individual needs. We provide a list of services to those living in Franklin, Fairfield, Licking, Muskingum and Perry Counties in Ohio. Our goals is to work with individuals, familes and staff to help improve the health and well-being of our clients. At Guardian Health Agency, we care about you.


We are currently taking new patients and their aides. If you do not have an aide, we provide one, or if you have family members, we can hire and train them to be your aide.


Our Trained staff consists of:

  1. Registered Nurses (RN)
  2. Licences Practical Nurses (LPN)
  3. State Tested Nursing Assistants (STNA)
  4. Home Health Aides
  5. Physical Therapists
  6. Occupational Therapists
  7. Speech Therapists

Our staff provide exceptional services in:

  1. Skilled Nursing
  2. Physical Therapy
  3. Occupational Therapy
  4. Speech Therapy
  5. Medical Social Services
  6. Case Management
  7. Home Health Aides
  8. 24/7 On-call Staff

We accept these insurances:

  1. Medicare Part A
  2. Medicare/Waiver
  3. DODD/MRDD
  4. Aetna
  5. Molina
  6. Passport and more
