About Us

Our mission is to nurture health to promote the welfare of individuals and families by providing compassionate and comprehensive patient-centered quality home health care that is safe and dependable.


At Guardian Angel Health Agency, we are committed to providing our clients with holistic, patient-centered care that is patient-driven and tailored to meet our client's individual needs. We provide a list of services to those living in Franklin, Fairfield, Licking, Muskingum, and Perry Counties in Ohio. Our goal is to work with patients, their families, and staff to help improve the health and well-being of our clients. At Guardian Angel Health Agency, we care about you.


Our Trained staff consists of:

  1. Registered Nurses (RN)
  2. Home Health Aides
  3. Licences Practical Nurses (LPN)
  4. State Tested Nursing Assistants (STNA)
  5. Speech Therapists
  6. Physical Therapists
  7. Occupational Therapists

Our staff provide services in:

  1. 24/7 On-call Staff
  2. Skilled Nursing
  3. Home Health Aides
  4. Physical Therapy
  5. Speech Therapy
  6. Case Management
  7. Occupational Therapy
